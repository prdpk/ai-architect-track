Motor Insurance Policy

Accidental damage to the insured vehicle is covered after a deductible of $750. This includes damage caused by collisions, overturning, and impact with stationary objects.

Theft of the insured vehicle is covered if a police report is filed within 24 hours. Personal belongings left inside the vehicle are not covered under this policy.

Third-party liability is covered up to $100,000 for bodily injury and property damage caused by the insured vehicle.

Damage caused by driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs, using the vehicle for racing, or driving without a valid licence is not covered.

Roadside assistance includes towing up to 50 kilometres, battery jump-start, flat-tyre assistance, and emergency fuel delivery.
