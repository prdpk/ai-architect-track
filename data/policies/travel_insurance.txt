Travel Insurance Policy

Emergency medical treatment during an overseas trip is covered up to $50,000. The policyholder must contact the emergency assistance provider as soon as reasonably possible.

Trip cancellation is covered when the journey is cancelled because of serious illness, accidental injury, or the death of the policyholder or an immediate family member.

Lost or stolen baggage is covered up to $2,000. Valuable items such as jewellery, cameras, and laptops are subject to a separate limit of $500 per item.

Travel delays longer than six hours may qualify for reimbursement of reasonable meals and accommodation expenses up to $300.

Pre-existing medical conditions, losses caused by unattended baggage, and travel to destinations under an official government travel warning are not covered.
